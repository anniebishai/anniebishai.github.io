About Me

I am a mission-driven, detail-oriented problem solver who brings curiosity and rigor to my work in data science. I specialize in using Python, machine learning, natural language processing, and data visualization to help organizations maximize their impact.

I am passionate about health access, environmental sustainability, and government accountability, and my data science portfolio reflects my dedication to creatively using data science for good. From analyzing language used on each side of the abortion debate, to modeling election outcomes based on counties' COVID vaccination rates, to examining the relationship between tree density and wealth in New York City's census tracts, my work is centered on gathering insights from data that can impact public life.
